Entrepreneurship Summit

ESummit'24 by
E-Cell NITK

The annual Entrepreneurship Summit more fondly called the E-Summit is the flagship event of E-Cell NITK. Essentially a celebration of the entrepreneurial spirit, it is a multi-faceted event that encompasses talks by eminent entrepreneurs, interactive workshops, business competitions and a pitching event where startups are given the chance to interact and connect with investors and mentors.

    Some Frequently Asked Questions

    What is ESummit'24?

    ESummit'24 is a premier event that brings together industry leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs to share insights, network, and explore the latest trends in technology, business, and startups.

    Who can attend ESummit'24?

    ESummit'24 is open to entrepreneurs, students, professionals, investors, and anyone interested in the fields of technology, business, and innovation. Whether you're a startup founder or a corporate leader, there's something for everyone.

    How can I register for ESummit'24?

    Registration for ESummit'24 can be completed online through our official website. Simply fill out the registration form, select your ticket type, and complete the payment process to secure your spot.

    What are the key highlights of ESummit'24?

    ESummit'24 will feature keynote speeches from industry leaders, panel discussions on current trends, workshops for skill development, networking sessions, and startup pitches. It's a comprehensive platform for learning, collaboration, and growth.

    Are there opportunities for networking at ESummit'24?

    Yes, ESummit'24 provides numerous networking opportunities, including dedicated networking sessions, meet-and-greet events, and a startup fair where participants can interact with entrepreneurs, investors, and industry experts.

    Will ESummit'24 be held online or in-person?

    ESummit'24 will be a hybrid event, offering both in-person and virtual participation options. This allows attendees from around the world to join and benefit from the event's content and networking opportunities.
